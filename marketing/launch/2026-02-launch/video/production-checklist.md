# Launch Video Production Checklist

## Pre-generation

- Confirm final USP copy lines
- Confirm brand name spelling: MutualWink
- Confirm URL: mutualwink.com
- Confirm aspect ratios needed: 16:9 master and 9:16 social

## Prompt completeness

- Narrative arc included (problem, product, trust, outcome)
- Required on-screen text included exactly
- Visual constraints included (no logos, no fake real profile screenshots)
- Tone and audience safety constraints included

## Output review

- Text readable on mobile and desktop
- End card visible and clear
- Emotional tone feels safe and hopeful
- No explicit trademark/platform references
- Diverse and respectful representation

## Export package

- 30s master (16:9)
- 15s cutdown (9:16)
- Optional hook-first version
- Thumbnail frame selected

## QA signoff

- Messaging aligns with product behavior
- USP statements are accurate for deployed backend behavior
- File names follow campaign naming convention
